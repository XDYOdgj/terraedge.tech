Downluxe Waterproof Clear Shower Curtain Liner

Keep your bathroom floor dry and clean with this sleek, modern, and highly functional clear shower curtain liner.

Features

  • Waterproof and easy to clean design
  • Lightweight PEVA material for durability
  • 3 weighted magnets for secure fit
  • Reinforced top header with 12 grommets
  • Clear shower curtain liner for a modern look

Why Choose This Product?

Crafted with high-quality PEVA material, this shower curtain liner is both waterproof and easy to clean, making it a reliable addition to any bathroom. The lightweight construction ensures comfort and ease of installation, while the reinforced top header comes with 12 metal grommets to keep it firmly in place.

The 3 weighted magnets at the bottom help anchor the liner to the tub, preventing it from shifting or blowing during use. This clear design seamlessly blends with most shower curtains, adding a touch of modern elegance to your bathroom decor.

Ideal for standard showers and bathtubs, this 72\" x 72\" liner works efficiently with both straight and curved shower rods. It’s not just functional—it’s built to last and enhance your space for years to come.
